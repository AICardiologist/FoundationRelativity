DESCRIPTION:
Paper 38 in the Constructive Reverse Mathematics of Mathematical Physics series. Every undecidability result in quantum many-body physics --- from spectral gaps to phase diagrams --- descends from the undecidability of Wang tiling (Berger 1966). This paper establishes that Wang tiling itself is Turing-Weihrauch equivalent to LPO, proving that the entire genealogy of physical undecidability from Berger through Cubitt lives at a single logical stratum.

The paper proves four theorems: (1) the Wang tiling decision problem is LPO-equivalent; (2) aperiodicity detection is LPO-equivalent; (3) the Genealogy Theorem: all 7 known undecidability results in mathematical physics (Berger 1966, Kanter 1990, GWPN 2009, CPgW 2015, BCLPG 2020, BCW 2021, CLPE 2022) factor through Wang tiling and are LPO-equivalent; (4) the Sigma-1-zero ceiling: no Sigma-1-zero-complete reduction can exceed LPO.

Note: Watson-Cubitt (2022) ground state energy density is excluded from the genealogy. It is a QMA-hardness (complexity) result for finite lattices, not an undecidability result. The ground state energy at finite volume is a BISH-computable finite eigenvalue problem (Paper 37).

All results formalized in Lean 4 over Mathlib with zero sorry. The formalization comprises 7 Lean source files (577 lines total). Lean 4 toolchain v4.28.0-rc1.
