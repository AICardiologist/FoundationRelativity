## Key Finding: Trace-Zero Form Invariant

For each totally real cubic F/Q (cyclic or non-cyclic), the sublattice
{x ∈ O_F : Tr_{F/Q}(x) = 0} carries a positive-definite binary
quadratic form (the 'trace-zero form') of discriminant -12·disc(F).

Its GL₂(Z)-equivalence class is a well-defined arithmetic invariant of F.
For cyclic cubics of conductor f, this form is 2f·(1,1,1).
For S₃ cubics, it is generically non-scalar and the form class
encodes the same information as the Weil lattice Gram form class.
